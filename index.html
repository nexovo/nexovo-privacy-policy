Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2025

Welcome to Nexovo ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve Nexovo’s services:

  • Personal Data: Including your UserId, full name, username, email address, age, gender, profile picture URL, location details (Area, City, Country), education, profession, religion, account status, verification status, joining date, user status (online/offline), balance, earnings, followers and following counts.
  • User Generated Content: Posts, comments, likes, stories, live stream video/audio, chat messages, premium post purchases, and other interactions within the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on the app, device type, IP address, log data, and analytics data collected through Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details related to in-app coin purchases through Google Play Billing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize Nexovo’s social media, live streaming, audio/video calls, chat, and posting services.
  • Manage user accounts, authentication, and verification using Firebase and Agora services.
  • Enable in-app purchases of coins and premium posts through Google Play Billing.
  • Show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob and improve ad experience.
  • Monitor and analyze app usage with Firebase Analytics to improve performance and features.
  • Moderate content: We allow users to report inappropriate or illegal content. Our team reviews reports and removes or bans content/users violating rules, including illegal, sexual, or harmful content.
  • Communicate important updates, notifications, and support messages.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, illegal activities, and ensure compliance with laws and app policies.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share data in the following limited cases:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party providers such as Firebase, Agora, Google AdMob, and Google Play services to operate and maintain Nexovo.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, we may disclose your information.
  • Protecting Rights: To protect Nexovo, our users, or others from harm, fraud, or illegal actions.

4. User Content Reporting and Moderation

Users can report posts, comments, or other content they find offensive, illegal, or violating community guidelines. Our moderation team reviews these reports. If content is found to violate rules (e.g., illegal, sexual content), we will remove it and may ban offending users to maintain a safe community.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Update: You can update your profile details anytime within the app.
  • Delete Account: You may request account deletion by contacting support.
  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads: You can adjust your ad preferences via device settings or Google Ad Settings.
  • Payment Management: Manage your in-app purchases and subscriptions through Google Play.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no online service is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Nexovo is intended for users aged 18 years or older. We do not allow registration for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a person under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

8. Data Retention

Your personal data and content are retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Upon account deletion, your data will be removed within a reasonable time.

9. Third-Party Services

Nexovo uses third-party services including Firebase, Agora, Google AdMob, and Google Play Billing. These providers have their own privacy policies, and your use of these services is also subject to their terms.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, the "Last updated" date above will change. Please review it periodically to stay informed.

11. Disclaimer and Limitation of Liability

While we strive to maintain a safe platform, Nexovo is not responsible for user-generated content or any illegal activity by users. Users are responsible for their own actions. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts violating our Terms.
